Nerdwire Tec is a nerd, enthusiast and advocate. The owner and operator of Nerdwire WIki, Nerdwire Publishing and Nerdwire Community.
Nerdwire Wiki serves as the webpage. Nerdwire Publishing serves as the ID used on the LBRY platform to post content. Nerdwire Community serves as the group name on platforms like MeWe and on Matrix/Riot.
Nerdwire Tec's goal and idea behind each project and platform is to provide a committed source for knowledgeable information of technology which is critical in today's digital environment through a broad array of solutions needed to keep your computers in operation with repair, graphic design, privacy, security, web development, social-media, consulting support and other. Allowing access to accurate content and information worldwide, regardless of where readers, listeners and or watchers live.
Nerdwire Tec's projects and ideas are independent and are run by people not by boards, not by stockholders, not by algorithms. When you support us via shares, likes, comments and or currency you do not help another millionaire buy a third vacation and or home. You help each project and or idea go forward. You help us help others as well as yourself.
We want to thank each and everyone of the contributors in advance and let you know all that you do is appreciated!
